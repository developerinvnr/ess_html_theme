Assistant Scientist - Biotechnology
Job Code VSPL/R&D/ASST.SC.BIOTECH/516-2024
Job Category Research & Development
Job Description

Job Title: Assistant Scientist - Biotechnology

Job Summary:

The Assistant Scientist in Biotechnology will contribute to research and development projects focused on the application of biotechnological methods in agriculture, medicine, or industrial processes. This role involves working closely with senior scientists and research teams to explore innovative solutions, optimize experimental protocols, and analyze data. The position requires a strong background in biotechnology, molecular biology, and laboratory techniques.

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Research and Experimentation:

    • Design and conduct experiments using biotechnological methods such as genetic engineering, CRISPR/Cas9, cloning, and recombinant DNA technology.
    • Develop and optimize protocols for DNA/RNA extraction, PCR, gene editing, protein expression, and other molecular biology techniques.
    • Analyze experimental data using bioinformatics tools, statistical software, and advanced imaging techniques.

  2. Project Management:

    • Assist in planning and managing research projects, including developing timelines, organizing resources, and ensuring adherence to project goals.
    • Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to integrate biotechnological approaches into broader research objectives.
    • Maintain detailed records of research activities, including laboratory notebooks, databases, and reports.

  3. Product Development:

    • Contribute to the development and optimization of biotechnological products, including genetically modified organisms (GMOs), biofuels, pharmaceuticals, and diagnostic tools.
    • Participate in scaling up laboratory protocols to pilot-scale production, ensuring reproducibility and quality control.

  4. Field and Laboratory Work:

    • Conduct laboratory experiments and, when necessary, field trials to validate biotechnological applications in real-world settings.
    • Collect, process, and analyze samples using various laboratory techniques.

  5. Publication and Reporting:

    • Prepare research findings for publication in scientific journals, patents, and presentations at conferences.
    • Write technical reports, grant proposals, and other documentation required by funding agencies and stakeholders.

  6. Supervision and Training:

    • Provide guidance and mentorship to junior researchers, laboratory technicians, and interns.
    • Train personnel in the use of specialized biotechnological equipment and techniques, ensuring adherence to safety protocols.

Qualifications:

  • Education: Ph.D. in Biotechnology, Molecular Biology, Genetic Engineering, or a related field. A Master's degree with significant research experience may also be considered.
  • Experience: Experience in a biotechnology research laboratory, with a focus on molecular biology, genetic engineering, or related areas.
  • Skills:
    • Proficiency in molecular biology techniques (e.g., gene cloning, CRISPR, qPCR, sequencing).
    • Familiarity with bioinformatics tools and software for data analysis.
    • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
    • Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a research team.
    • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with product development and commercialization of biotechnological innovations.
  • Knowledge of regulatory guidelines and ethical considerations in biotechnology.
  • Experience in grant writing and securing research funding.

Work Environment:

  • Primarily laboratory-based work, with occasional fieldwork depending on the nature of the research.
  • Some travel may be required for conferences, collaboration, and field studies.
Desired Candidate Profile
Education Qualification
  • Master of Science - Biotechnology
  • Doctor of Philosophy - Molecular Biology & Biotechnology
Work Experience 0-3
